Urban Planning & Infrastructure

AEX has a proven history in managing urban planning and infrastructure projects.

AEX boasts a proven track record in urban planning and infrastructure projects, demonstrating our expertise and reliability in delivering high-quality solutions.

AEX’s urban planning and infrastructure team specializes in addressing the multifaceted challenges of contemporary urban environments. We focus on delivering design excellence, supported by years of experience and unparalleled technical expertise.

In urban planning, both visionary foresight and practical pragmatism are essential for achieving success. To meet requirements effectively, it is crucial to meticulously consider planning codes, environmental impact, landscape integration, and financial feasibility. Coupled with rigorous project management and precise scheduling, these elements are key to fulfilling our clients' objectives.

Key experience encompasses comprehensive master planning, advanced urban design infrastructure, integrated transportation solutions, innovative new city planning, and the revitalization of brownfield sites.

Urban development necessitates a comprehensive and integrated approach, and AEX is ideally positioned to deliver. Our capabilities span from transport planning to public space design, offering innovative traffic solutions and strategic planning for future growth and evolution.

AEX management team brings a wealth of experience and confidence, ensuring clarity, quality assurance, and sustainable development for long-term economic viability.

"Leveraging extensive experience in managing intricate projects, we provide expert solutions through a comprehensive and integrated approach."
