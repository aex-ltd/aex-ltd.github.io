Supervision and Owner Representation

In the realm of Construction Supervision and Owner Representation, we leverage the extensive experience of our partners, EXP and AAA, along with the expertise of LACC, a principal shareholder in AEX. EXP and AAA bring a wealth of knowledge from their extensive portfolio of large-scale and complex projects, forming the bedrock of our superior oversight and representation services.

The highly qualified team at LACC, in complete synergy with AAA, EXP, and the AEX team, ensures that every aspect of your project—whether it involves building design, infrastructure, energy, or environmental considerations—is managed with the utmost precision and professionalism. This collaborative approach allows us to provide comprehensive supervision and representation, securing your interests and upholding the highest standards throughout the construction process.

Our services include:

Construction Supervision ▶

Our Construction Supervision services ensure that every aspect of the construction process is managed with the highest level of quality, safety, and efficiency. With the combined expertise of LACC, EXP, AAA, and AEX, we provide comprehensive oversight throughout the project. Acting as the intermediary between the client and the contractor, our main goal is to ensure that the project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards, while minimizing issues, claims, and problems. We focus on adhering to design specifications, safety regulations, and contractual obligations, conducting regular site inspections, monitoring progress, and coordinating effectively with all parties involved to guarantee smooth and successful project delivery.

Our services include:

Review of Contract Documents ▶

We meticulously review all contract documents, including specifications, drawings, and agreements, to ensure they align with project requirements and legal standards. This comprehensive review process helps identify and resolve any discrepancies or ambiguities before construction begins.

Site Inspections ▶

Regular site inspections are conducted to monitor construction progress, verify adherence to design specifications, and ensure quality standards are met. Our inspections provide real-time feedback, enabling prompt corrective actions to address any issues that arise during construction.

Quality Control ▶

We implement rigorous quality control procedures to ensure that all construction activities meet the highest standards of quality. Our quality control measures include detailed inspections, testing of materials, and verification of workmanship to ensure that every aspect of the project is executed to perfection.

Safety Compliance ▶

Ensuring safety compliance is a top priority. We oversee safety protocols and procedures on-site to protect workers, visitors, and the public. Our team ensures that all safety regulations are followed, and we conduct regular safety audits to address potential hazards and maintain a safe working environment.

Progress Monitoring ▶

We closely monitor project progress against the planned schedule to ensure timely completion. Our progress monitoring involves tracking milestones, reviewing work completed, and addressing any delays or issues that could impact the project timeline.

Coordination with Contractors ▶

Effective coordination with contractors is crucial for smooth project execution. We facilitate communication between all parties involved, ensuring that contractors adhere to project plans, resolve issues efficiently, and maintain a collaborative approach throughout the construction process.

Change Management ▶

Our change management process addresses any changes to the project scope, design, or schedule. We evaluate the impact of proposed changes, implement necessary adjustments, and manage change orders to ensure that modifications are effectively integrated into the project.

Issue Resolution ▶

We proactively address and resolve any issues or conflicts that arise during construction. Our issue resolution process involves identifying problems, analyzing potential solutions, and implementing corrective actions to minimize disruptions and keep the project on track.

Documentation and Reporting ▶

Comprehensive documentation and reporting are essential for project transparency and accountability. We maintain detailed records of all construction activities, inspections, and decisions, providing regular reports to stakeholders and ensuring that all project documentation is complete and accurate.

Compliance Verification ▶

We verify that all aspects of the construction project comply with relevant regulations, codes, and standards. Our compliance verification process includes reviewing documentation, conducting inspections, and ensuring that all legal and contractual requirements are met.

Test and Inspection Coordination ▶

Coordinating tests and inspections is critical to ensuring that materials and workmanship meet required standards. We schedule and manage testing procedures, review results, and address any issues identified during inspections to maintain high quality and compliance.

Submittal Review ▶

We review all submittals from contractors, including material samples, shop drawings, and product specifications. Our review process ensures that all submittals comply with project requirements and design intent before approval and implementation.

Deficiency Management ▶

Our deficiency management process involves identifying, tracking, and resolving any deficiencies or incomplete work. We address issues promptly to ensure that all aspects of the project meet the required standards and specifications before final completion.

Validation of Shop Drawings ▶

We validate shop drawings to ensure they accurately reflect the design intent and specifications. Our validation process involves checking drawings for compliance with project requirements and coordinating with contractors to resolve any discrepancies.

Validation of Materials ▶

The validation of materials ensures that all construction materials meet project specifications and quality standards. We verify material certifications, conduct tests, and inspect deliveries to ensure that materials used are suitable for their intended purpose and comply with project requirements.

Contractual Obligations Enforcement ▶

We enforce contractual obligations to ensure that all parties adhere to their contractual commitments. Our enforcement process involves monitoring compliance with terms and conditions, addressing breaches, and ensuring that all contractual requirements are met.

Final Inspection and Handover ▶

The final inspection and handover process ensures that the completed project meets all requirements and is ready for occupancy or use. We conduct a thorough final inspection, address any remaining issues, and facilitate the official handover of the project to the client, ensuring a smooth transition and project completion.

Owner Representation and Liaison ▶

Our Owner Representation and Liaison services ensure that the client’s interests are effectively represented and managed throughout the construction process. Acting as the client’s advocate, we facilitate clear communication and coordination between the client, contractors, designers, supervision consultants, and relevant authorities. Our primary goal is to ensure that the project meets the client’s vision, stays on schedule, remains within budget, and adheres to the highest quality standards, while minimizing risks and resolving any issues that arise.

We provide expert oversight in financial management, including budget control, finance, and cash flow monitoring, to ensure that all financial aspects of the project are managed efficiently. By bridging communication gaps and coordinating with all stakeholders, we help navigate complex project dynamics and maintain a smooth workflow.
