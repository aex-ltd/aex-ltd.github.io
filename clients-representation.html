Clients & Project Management Division

The Client Representation and Project Management Division is staffed with project managers, client relationship managers, risk managers, and resource coordinators. Team members typically hold degrees in project management, business administration, or engineering, and possess certifications such as PMP (Project Management Professional), PRINCE2 (Projects IN Controlled Environments), and PMI-RMP (Project Management Institute - Risk Management Professional). The division employs advanced project management software such as Microsoft Project, Primavera P6, and Smartsheet for planning and scheduling, as well as collaboration tools like Asana and Trello. Risk management is supported by tools like Palisade @RISK, while financial tracking is handled using software such as QuickBooks and SAP. Our personnel in Nigeria ensure real-time, day-to-day coordination with international consultants, maintaining a direct and highly professional link to our clients. The role of AEX is to complete the missions by itself or with our international consultants EXP and AAA by assuring the perfect, professional, and easy coordination between the client, AEX, and our international consultants to ensure that all missions are completed to the entire satisfaction of the client. This experienced and technologically equipped team ensures comprehensive client representation and efficient project delivery.
