Quality Control

Uncompromising Quality Standards

To ensure outstanding quality at every stage of a project, AEX integrates quality assurance and quality control into all our undertakings. We offer inspection, engineering, and quality control services in a reliable, independent, and impartial manner, adhering to standards and specifications without compromising our core values.

  • Quality control is our primary concern and an integral part of our business.
  • AEX upholds high standards for quality in accordance with ISO recommendations.
  • Our Project Managers, guided by our empowerment policy, implement Quality System Procedures across all our design units and joint ventures.
  • At the completion of each project, lessons learned are thoroughly documented and archived.
  • The improvement of our work methodology, both technical and managerial, is an ongoing process.
  • We advocate for cutting-edge technical, professional, and safety standards.
  • We strive to be part of the conversation, actively seeking to influence industry thinking.

    • Quality control has been at the heart of AEX's practice since our inception. By placing program controls and management at the center of our specialized services, we continuously update our checklists to meet ever-evolving codes, passing on the benefits to our clients. Through collaboration, training, and a thorough understanding of processes and procedures, AEX’s quality control and recommendations transform the most demanding requirements into project success.

    "The primary goal of every project is to achieve a high-quality outcome while staying within budget and on schedule. AEX's in-house quality control expertise ensures that projects remain on track from start to completion."
