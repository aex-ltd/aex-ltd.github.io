Welcome to AEX

AEX is a leading international consultancy group specializing in financial engineering, design, supervision, and project management. Driven by excellence and innovation, AEX is dedicated to delivering outstanding results in every project we undertake.

For over 80 years, our group of engineers and partners has been providing comprehensive solutions and services across financial engineering, buildings design, infrastructure design, construction supervision, and project management. We ensure the highest standards and pioneering advancements in all our projects.

At AEX, we are proud to collaborate with renowned partners such as EXP, our infrastructure partner, AAA, our architecture and buildings partner, and ACIF-JDS, our financial partner. Together, we combine our expertise to deliver unparalleled service and innovative solutions to our clients.

Operating in Nigeria, our offices are strategically located to serve the nation's diverse needs. Our expatriate, experienced personnel are based in Nigeria, working alongside our highly qualified local team. This ensures that you can directly communicate with us to gather information, develop ideas, and promptly solve issues. We are committed to making Nigeria better through our dedicated services.

Whether you are a developer seeking a professional consultant to design your project or need supervision services, AEX is here to assist you. If you are an agency, government, or state entity looking to design and supervise roads, dams, or other infrastructure projects, we offer expert design and supervision services.

For investors aiming to maximize their investment profit, AEX provides comprehensive financial engineering, business planning, and other consulting services to turn your vision into reality.

Explore our website to learn more about our services and how we can help you achieve your project goals.
