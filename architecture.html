Architecture

We Craft Excellence In Every Detail

Founded in 1965, AEX has been committed to delivering seamless architectural and engineering services from its inception. Our integrated practice enables technologically advanced designs that are appropriate, economical, and sensitive to both environmental and human needs.

Our practice has an established track record for tackling large and complex challenges. With our comprehensive offering of advanced skills and multidisciplinary teams, AEX is uniquely well-placed to design technically demanding and functional buildings.

Through extensive experience with large multidisciplinary projects, AEX has developed its design team to bring clarity and focus, ensuring that project and client objectives are prioritized. This approach guarantees the efficient completion of projects on time and within budget.

"Our architecture studios help developers deliver stylish, functional buildings with optimal environmental, energy, financial, and social performance."
