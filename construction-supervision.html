Construction Supervision Division

The Construction Supervision Division is dedicated to ensuring the successful execution of building and infrastructure projects, adhering to the highest standards outlined in FIDIC contracts, other national and international models, and in full compliance with Nigerian law. Our division combines the extensive experience of our in-house personnel with the global expertise of EXP and AAA, delivering comprehensive supervision services that encompass all technical, financial, planning, and contractual aspects of a project.

Our division is equipped to oversee every facet of construction, whether it involves building or infrastructure projects. We have a multidisciplinary team of professionals, including civil, electrical, and mechanical engineers, architects, industrial engineers, quantity surveyors, cost control experts, planning specialists, quality control and quality assurance personnel, and Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) officers. This diverse team ensures that every element of the project is meticulously managed, from design integrity to contractor performance and compliance with contractual obligations.

We serve as the vital link between the client (owner representative) and the contractor, ensuring that communication is clear, consistent, and effective. Our mission is to guarantee that the project progresses according to the clientâ€™s objectives, maintaining the integrity of the design while promptly addressing any necessary adjustments. We provide rigorous oversight to ensure that the contractor adheres strictly to the contract terms, with a strong focus on quality, safety, and efficiency.

Our role as the intermediary between the client and the contractor is crucial in facilitating smooth project execution. We ensure that all parties are aligned, that decisions are made collaboratively, and that any issues are resolved swiftly to avoid delays or misunderstandings. By integrating the expertise of EXP and AAA with our own, the Construction Supervision Division ensures that every aspect of a project is managed with precision and professionalism, leading to successful outcomes that meet or exceed client expectations.
