Strategic Business & Finance Division

The Strategic Business and Finance Division is staffed with experienced project managers, financial analysts, investment advisors, and market researchers. Personnel typically hold advanced degrees in finance, business administration, economics, or related fields and have extensive experience in project identification, market analysis, and financial planning. The division leverages advanced financial software such as Bloomberg Terminal, Microsoft Excel for financial modeling, SPSS for statistical analysis, and Oracle Hyperion for financial management. Innovative tools like Tableau and Power BI are used for data visualization and market insights. Additionally, our experienced personnel, present in Nigeria, ensure real-time, day-to-day coordination with ACIF-JDS in Montreal, maintaining a highly professional link to our clients. The role of AEX is to complete the missions by itself or with our international consultants EXP and AAA by assuring the perfect, professional, and easy coordination between the client, AEX, and our international consultants to ensure that all missions are completed to the entire satisfaction of the client. This combination of expertise and cutting-edge technology, coupled with our exclusive representation of ACIF-JDS in Nigeria, ensures robust financial strategies and successful project outcomes.


  • Identifying Opportunities : Research and identify potential projects that align with Clients/Investors objectives, leveraging market insights and industry trends. Develop a pipeline of viable project opportunities to present to Clients/Investors. Use various sources such as industry reports, market research, and networking to uncover new opportunities. Continuously monitor the market to stay updated on emerging trends and potential projects. Analyze and assess the viability of these opportunities for implementation in Nigeria.
  • Conducting market analysis : Analyze market trends, competitive landscapes, and economic factors to inform strategic project decisions and client recommendations. Provide insights to guide project selection and investment. Use quantitative and qualitative data to assess market conditions. Prepare detailed market analysis reports that highlight opportunities and risks. Present findings to clients to support informed decision-making. Evaluate client-proposed projects to verify market suitability and potential success.
  • Evaluating project feasibility : Assess the technical, financial, and operational feasibility of projects to ensure viability and alignment with client goals. Conduct detailed feasibility studies to support decision-making. Consider factors such as site conditions, regulatory requirements, and market demand. Use feasibility assessments to guide project planning and risk management. Verify client-proposed projects for feasibility and practicality. Provide recommendations for improvements or modifications as needed.
  • Improving developer proposals : Enhance developer proposals with strategic insights, financial analysis, and expert recommendations to maximize project success. Collaborate with developers to refine project plans. Ensure proposals are comprehensive, well-structured, and aligned with client objectives. Provide feedback and support to developers to enhance proposal quality. Assess client-provided proposals for completeness and alignment with best practices. Suggest improvements to optimize project outcomes.
  • Preparing project proposals : Develop comprehensive, compelling proposals that clearly outline project scope, benefits, and financial details to secure client approvals and investments. Tailor proposals to meet client-specific needs. Use persuasive language and clear visuals to present proposals effectively. Ensure all necessary documentation and supporting information are included. Collaborate with various stakeholders to gather relevant data and insights. Review and revise proposals to address client feedback and requirements.
  • Creating business plans : Formulate detailed business plans that outline project objectives, strategies, market analysis, and financial projections. Ensure plans are robust and aligned with client goals. Use business planning tools and frameworks to structure plans. Include risk assessments and contingency strategies in business plans. Collaborate with financial experts to develop realistic financial projections. Present business plans to clients and stakeholders for approval.
  • Creating financial plans : Design robust financial plans that include budgeting, forecasting, funding strategies, and financial risk management. Develop plans that ensure financial stability and profitability. Use financial modeling and analysis tools to create accurate financial plans. Incorporate sensitivity analysis to test different financial scenarios. Review and update financial plans regularly to reflect changing conditions. Present financial plans to clients and stakeholders for review.
  • Developing investment strategies : Identify and implement investment approaches to optimize returns, manage risks, and ensure financial sustainability for projects. Develop tailored investment strategies for each project. Use a mix of traditional and innovative investment approaches. Monitor and adjust investment strategies as needed based on market conditions. Collaborate with investment advisors to identify suitable opportunities. Present investment strategies to clients for approval.
  • Structuring financial deals : Negotiate and structure financial arrangements, including equity and debt financing, to secure necessary funding for projects. Ensure deals are favorable and meet project needs. Work with legal and financial advisors to structure deals. Prepare detailed financial models and term sheets to support deal negotiations. Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards. Finalize financial agreements with all parties involved.
  • Developing financing models : Create comprehensive financing models to support project funding, sustainability, and financial planning. Use advanced financial modeling techniques to forecast project financials. Ensure models are flexible and can accommodate different financing scenarios. Use financing models to support decision-making and investor presentations. Collaborate with financial experts to validate model assumptions. Regularly update models to reflect current data and projections.
  • Securing investors : Identify, engage, and secure potential investors to provide necessary capital for project development and execution. Build and maintain relationships with investors. Use targeted marketing and outreach strategies to attract investors. Provide regular updates and reports to investors to maintain their confidence. Organize investor meetings and presentations to showcase project opportunities. Ensure clear communication and transparency with all investors.
  • Securing project financing : Collaborate with financial institutions and partners to secure project financing, ensuring all funding requirements are met. Develop financing packages that attract and satisfy investors. Negotiate favorable financing terms and conditions. Ensure all financing agreements are documented and compliant with regulations. Monitor the disbursement and use of funds to ensure alignment with project budgets. Provide regular financial updates to clients and stakeholders.
