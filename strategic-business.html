Strategic Business & Finance Division

The Strategic Business and Finance Division is a key driver of financial excellence and strategic project success. Our team is composed of seasoned professionals, including project managers, financial analysts, investment advisors, and market researchers, all of whom bring extensive expertise in project identification, market analysis, and financial planning. Each team member typically holds advanced degrees in finance, business administration, economics, or related fields, ensuring that our division is equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate complex financial landscapes.

We employ a suite of advanced financial tools to deliver precise and insightful analysis. This includes the use of Bloomberg Terminal for real-time financial data, Microsoft Excel for sophisticated financial modeling, SPSS for in-depth statistical analysis, and Oracle Hyperion for comprehensive financial management. Our commitment to innovation is further demonstrated through the use of Tableau and Power BI for data visualization, enabling us to transform complex data into clear, actionable insights.

Our division also benefits from a strong partnership with ACIF-JDS in Montreal, ensuring that our operations in Nigeria are seamlessly aligned with global financial standards and practices. Our experienced personnel in Nigeria coordinate closely with ACIF-JDS, providing real-time, day-to-day oversight and maintaining a highly professional link to our clients. This collaborative approach allows us to deliver robust financial strategies that are tailored to meet the unique challenges and opportunities of each project.
