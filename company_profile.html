Company Profile

COMPANY PROFILE

As a premier Group of International Firms specializing in Architecture, Design, Engineering, Infrastructure and Project Management Consultancy, AEX is committed to excellence and innovation.

For over 80 years, our Group has provided comprehensive solutions and services across the entire Architecture and Consulting Engineering sector, ensuring the highest standards and cutting-edge advancements in every undertaken Infrastructure or Building Project.

Today, AEX stands as a beacon of engineering excellence in Nigeria, specializing in infrastructure and building projects. Powered by the collective experience of thousands of engineers from our strategic partners, EXP, a premier consultancy from Canada, and AAA, a distinguished architectural firm from Lebanon, AEX is uniquely positioned to secure and execute high-caliber projects across the region. Our experts, led by Engineer Jean-Paul Kassis, are always present in Nigeria, ready to coordinate with our valued clients at all times. Supported by our dedicated team of professionals, AEX continues to flourish, upholding an unrivaled reputation for quality and innovation in the industry.

At EXP, we are driven by passion – unified by our vision to deliver exponential possibilities for our clients, our employees and our communities. We are a full-service, multidisciplinary firm delivering projects and solutions to clients and communities around the world. Our heritage dates back to 1906, when the earliest of EXP’s predecessor companies started its engineering infrastructure practice. Today, thousands of creative professionals across EXP work together to deliver extraordinary experiences year after year.

At AAA we believe that buildings have to serve as a bridge between nature, culture and people, and that inspiring surroundings have a positive effect on people’s lives. More than 40 years of activity enabled AAA to design and supervise the planning and construction of more than 250 projects in many fields.

"Driven by excellence and innovation, our experts deliver comprehensive solutions and services that encompass the entire architecture and consulting engineering sectors."
