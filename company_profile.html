Who We Are

Who We Are

As a premier Group of Financials, Engineers and International Firms specializing in Fianncial Engineering, Architecture, Design, Engineering, Infrastructure and Project Management Consultancy, AEX is committed to excellence and innovation.

For over 80 years, our Group of Engineers and Partners has provided comprehensive solutions and services across the entire Architecture and Consulting Engineering sector, ensuring the highest standards and cutting-edge advancements in every undertaken Infrastructure or Building Project.

Today, AEX stands as a beacon of engineering excellence in Nigeria, specializing in infrastructure and building projects. Powered by the collective experience of thousands of engineers from our strategic partners, EXP, a premier consultancy from Canada, AAA, a distinguished architectural firm from Lebanon, and ACIF-JDS, a Canadian financial partner, AEX is uniquely positioned to secure and execute high-caliber projects across the region. Our experts are always present in Nigeria, ready to coordinate with our valued clients at all times. Supported by our dedicated team of professionals, AEX continues to flourish, upholding an unrivaled reputation for quality and innovation in the industry.

With EXP, we are driven by passion – unified by our vision to deliver exponential possibilities for our clients, our employees and our communities. EXP is a full-service, multidisciplinary firm delivering projects and solutions to clients and communities around the world. EXP heritage dates back to 1906, when the earliest of EXP’s predecessor companies started its engineering infrastructure practice. Today, thousands of creative professionals across EXP work together to deliver extraordinary experiences year after year.

With AAA we believe that buildings have to serve as a bridge between nature, culture and people, and that inspiring surroundings have a positive effect on people’s lives. More than 40 years of activity enabled AAA to design and supervise the planning and construction of more than 250 projects in many fields.

With ACIF-JDS , we believe that financial engineering is a critical tool for transforming visions into reality. With a global reach and a commitment to excellence, ACIF-JDS has been providing innovative financial solutions for over 40 years. Our expertise spans worldwide, enabling us to design and implement financial strategies that drive success in diverse projects and sectors.

"Driven by excellence and innovation, our experts deliver comprehensive solutions and services that encompass the entire architecture and consulting engineering sectors."

Our Practice

AEX's practice is rooted in a holistic approach, guiding projects from conception through to completion. Our strong creative teams collaborate seamlessly with structural and environmental engineers, supported by a wide array of in-house disciplines. This ensures that robust expertise and resources are always available to deliver exceptional results.

AEX collaborates closely with clients to develop solutions that are functional, constructible, and address the challenges of today. Our unwavering commitment to maximizing benefits and delivering projects on schedule is a testament to our sustained success for over 80 years.
