Infrastructure Design Division

The Infrastructure Design Division comprises civil engineers, structural engineers, environmental consultants, and CAD technicians. Team members typically hold degrees in civil engineering, environmental engineering, or related disciplines, and many possess professional certifications such as PE (Professional Engineer) or LEED AP (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design Accredited Professional). The division utilizes advanced design and analysis software including AutoCAD, Revit, Civil 3D, and Bentley MicroStation for drafting and modeling, as well as software like STAAD.Pro and SAP2000 for structural analysis. Environmental assessments are conducted using tools like GIS (Geographic Information System) and ENVI. Furthermore, our personnel on the ground in Nigeria ensure continuous, real-time coordination with international consultants, guaranteeing seamless integration and direct client communication. The role of AEX is to complete the missions by itself or with our international consultants EXP and AAA by assuring the perfect, professional, and easy coordination between the client, AEX, and our international consultants to ensure that all missions are completed to the entire satisfaction of the client. This combination of highly skilled personnel and sophisticated software ensures innovative and sustainable infrastructure solutions.


  • Conducting site surveys and assessments : Perform detailed site surveys and assessments to gather data on topography, soil conditions, and environmental factors. Use this data to inform design decisions. Employ advanced surveying techniques and equipment. Document and analyze survey findings to support design development. Coordinate with local authorities to obtain necessary permits and approvals. Address any site-specific challenges identified during surveys.
  • Preparing preliminary design concepts : Develop initial design concepts and plans that address project requirements and site conditions. Create innovative and practical solutions that meet project goals. Use sketching, modeling, and visualization tools to create preliminary designs. Collaborate with clients and stakeholders to refine design concepts. Incorporate feedback to ensure designs align with client expectations and regulatory requirements.
  • Developing detailed engineering designs : Create comprehensive engineering designs, including structural, civil, and environmental aspects, ensuring compliance with standards. Use advanced design tools and software. Ensure designs are practical, cost-effective, and sustainable. Conduct peer reviews and quality checks on engineering designs. Collaborate with other engineering disciplines to integrate all aspects of the design.
  • Coordinating with environmental consultants : Work with environmental consultants to incorporate sustainable practices and regulatory compliance into designs. Ensure designs minimize environmental impact. Integrate environmental assessments and impact studies into design processes. Collaborate on developing mitigation and enhancement measures. Ensure all environmental regulations and standards are met.
  • Ensuring compliance with local regulations and standards : Verify that all designs meet local building codes, regulations, and industry standards. Conduct thorough reviews and audits. Keep up-to-date with regulatory changes and updates. Implement compliance checklists and protocols in the design process. Ensure all necessary approvals and permits are obtained before construction begins.
  • Integrating sustainable design practices : Incorporate sustainable and eco-friendly practices into design solutions to minimize environmental impact. Use green building techniques and materials. Conduct sustainability assessments and certifications. Promote energy efficiency, water conservation, and waste reduction in designs. Ensure all designs meet relevant sustainability standards and certifications.
  • Collaborating with other design disciplines : Coordinate with geotechnical, structural, and other design disciplines to ensure cohesive and integrated project designs. Facilitate interdisciplinary teamwork. Use integrated design tools and platforms to enhance collaboration. Conduct regular coordination meetings and workshops. Ensure all design elements are harmonized and align with overall project objectives.
  • Preparing construction documents and specifications : Develop detailed construction documents, including drawings and technical specifications, for contractor use. Ensure documents are accurate and clear. Use standardized templates and formats for consistency. Review and update construction documents as needed. Ensure all documents comply with regulatory and industry standards.
  • Conducting design reviews and value engineering : Review designs for accuracy and efficiency, implementing value engineering to optimize project costs. Identify opportunities for cost savings without sacrificing quality. Use value engineering workshops and techniques. Document and implement value engineering recommendations. Ensure all stakeholders are involved in the value engineering process.
  • Providing technical support during construction : Offer technical assistance and guidance during the construction phase to address design-related issues. Ensure designs are implemented correctly on-site. Conduct site visits and inspections. Provide timely responses to construction queries and issues. Collaborate with construction teams to resolve any design-related challenges.
  • Coordinating with utility companies and stakeholders : Ensure alignment with utility companies and stakeholders to facilitate smooth project execution. Manage stakeholder communications and approvals. Coordinate utility relocations and connections. Address stakeholder concerns and feedback promptly. Ensure all utility requirements are integrated into the design and construction process.
  • Ensuring quality control and assurance in design deliverables : Implement quality control measures to maintain high standards in all design deliverables. Conduct regular audits and inspections. Use quality management systems and tools. Ensure continuous improvement in design processes and outcomes. Document and address any quality issues promptly to maintain project standards.
