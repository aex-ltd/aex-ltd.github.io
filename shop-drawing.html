Shop Drawings

"AEX seasoned professionals meticulously develop plans, ensuring optimal efficiency and precision throughout the review and construction phases."

As a premier international consultancy in architecture, design, engineering, and project management, AEX has been synonymous with excellence and innovation for over 50 years. We provide comprehensive solutions and services that encompass the entire spectrum of architecture and consulting engineering, ensuring the highest standards of quality and expertise.

AEX provides meticulously accurate and error-free shop drawings that stand out for their clarity, insightful detail, and purposeful design.

Shop drawings are a critical element of every construction project, offering indispensable and precise information about specifications and components, whether prefabricated or custom-built. These detailed drawings empower contractors and developers to achieve optimal performance and efficiency during the construction phase.

High-quality shop drawings are essential for envisioning the performance of a structure during the early design phase. By leveraging the detailed information provided in these drawings, architects and builders can meticulously plan and execute optimal construction processes

Shop drawings serve as a critical verification tool, ensuring that contractors, manufacturers, and suppliers fully comprehend the project's specifications and requirements, thereby preempting any potential misinterpretations.
