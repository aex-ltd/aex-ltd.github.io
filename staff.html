Staff & Organization

Staff & Organization

AEX understands that best practice comes from a holistic approach, guiding projects from conception through to completion. Our vastly experienced teams collaborate seamlessly from the planning stage to execution, ensuring integrated and efficient project delivery.

Our project management teams provide clients and end-users with confidence, enabling AEX to deliver an integrated service throughout the entire project lifecycle—from concept and build-up to final delivery and completion.

AEX places quality control at the heart of our practice, while effective budgeting, cost management, and resource management ensure a stable framework for operations. Additionally, AEX offers independent specialized services, assisting clients across the Middle East and globally in outlining, preparing, organizing, monitoring, auditing, and delivering projects efficiently and on schedule.


As a premier Group of International Firms specializing in Architecture, Design, Engineering, Infrastructure and Project Management Consultancy, AEX is committed to excellence and innovation.
