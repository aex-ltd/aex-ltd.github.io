Architectural Design Division

The Architectural Design Division at AEX comprises architects, interior designers, landscape architects, and urban planners with degrees and certifications like RA and AIA membership. They use advanced design software such as AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, Rhino, V-Ray, Lumion, LEED Online, and Sefaira. Personnel in Nigeria ensure real-time coordination with international consultants, maintaining a professional client link. Collaborating with EXP and AAA, AEX ensures missions are completed efficiently and to client satisfaction. This blend of creative talent and advanced technology results in innovative, sustainable, and aesthetically pleasing architectural solutions.

Conducting site analysis and feasibility studies ▶

Assess site conditions and conduct feasibility studies to determine project viability and potential challenges. Use findings to inform design strategies. Analyze site constraints, opportunities, and regulatory requirements. Prepare detailed feasibility reports and recommendations. Collaborate with clients to ensure alignment with project goals and site conditions. Incorporate feasibility study findings into the design process.

Developing architectural concepts and schematics ▶

Create initial architectural concepts and schematic designs that meet client requirements and site constraints. Use creative and functional design approaches. Employ sketching, modeling, and visualization tools. Collaborate with clients and stakeholders to refine design concepts. Ensure designs align with project goals and regulatory requirements. Incorporate client feedback to enhance design outcomes.

Creating detailed architectural designs and drawings ▶

Develop comprehensive architectural designs, including floor plans, elevations, and sections, adhering to client specifications. Use state-of-the-art design software. Ensure designs are practical, aesthetically pleasing, and functional. Conduct peer reviews and quality checks on architectural designs. Integrate feedback from stakeholders to refine and finalize designs. Ensure designs meet all relevant codes and standards.

Ensuring compliance with building codes and regulations ▶

Verify that all architectural designs comply with relevant building codes, regulations, and zoning laws. Conduct thorough reviews and ensure adherence. Keep up-to-date with regulatory changes and updates. Implement compliance checklists and protocols in the design process. Ensure all necessary permits and approvals are obtained before construction begins. Address any compliance issues promptly to maintain project schedules.

Integrating sustainable and green building practices ▶

Incorporate sustainable and eco-friendly practices into architectural designs to enhance energy efficiency and environmental performance. Use LEED and other green building standards. Conduct sustainability assessments and certifications. Promote energy efficiency, water conservation, and waste reduction in designs. Ensure all designs meet relevant sustainability standards and certifications. Collaborate with sustainability consultants to enhance design outcomes.

Coordinating with structural and MEP engineers ▶

Collaborate with structural, mechanical, electrical, and plumbing engineers to ensure integrated and cohesive design solutions. Facilitate interdisciplinary coordination. Use integrated design tools and platforms to enhance collaboration. Conduct regular coordination meetings and workshops. Ensure all design elements are harmonized and align with overall project objectives. Address any coordination issues promptly to maintain project schedules.

Preparing construction documents and specifications ▶

Produce detailed construction documents, including architectural drawings and technical specifications, for contractor use. Ensure clarity and accuracy. Use standardized templates and formats for consistency. Review and update construction documents as needed. Ensure all documents comply with regulatory and industry standards. Collaborate with contractors to address any construction document-related issues.

Conducting design reviews and revisions ▶

Review and refine architectural designs based on client feedback and project requirements, ensuring accuracy and feasibility. Iterate designs to meet evolving needs. Conduct design review meetings and workshops. Document and implement design revisions. Ensure all stakeholders are involved in the design review process. Address any design issues promptly to maintain project schedules.

Managing client presentations and feedback ▶

Present architectural designs to clients, gather feedback, and make necessary adjustments to meet client expectations. Ensure client satisfaction through effective communication. Use visual aids and presentation tools to enhance client presentations. Document and address client feedback promptly. Maintain clear and open communication with clients throughout the design process. Ensure all client requirements and preferences are incorporated into the final design.

Collaborating with interior designers and landscape architects ▶

Work with interior designers and landscape architects to create holistic and aesthetically pleasing design solutions. Integrate interior and exterior design elements. Coordinate design concepts and plans. Ensure cohesive and complementary design outcomes. Conduct regular coordination meetings and workshops. Address any coordination issues promptly to maintain project schedules.

Providing technical support during the construction phase ▶

Offer technical assistance and guidance during construction to address design-related issues and ensure project alignment. Ensure design integrity during implementation. Conduct site visits and inspections. Provide timely responses to construction queries and issues. Collaborate with construction teams to resolve any design-related challenges. Ensure all design elements are implemented as intended.

Ensuring quality control and assurance in design deliverables ▶

Implement quality control measures to maintain high standards in all architectural design deliverables. Conduct regular audits and ensure continuous improvement. Use quality management systems and tools. Ensure all deliverables meet or exceed client expectations. Document and address any quality issues promptly to maintain project standards. Ensure continuous improvement in design processes and outcomes.
