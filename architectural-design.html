Architectural & Building Design Division

The Building Design Division is a multidisciplinary powerhouse, staffed by a diverse team of professionals including architects, interior designers, landscape architects, urban planners, civil engineers, electrical engineers, mechanical engineers, industrial engineers, and urban designers. All members of the team hold advanced degrees in their respective fields, with many possessing professional licensure and certifications such as RA (Registered Architect), PE (Professional Engineer), and LEED AP (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design Accredited Professional).

Our in-house capabilities cover every aspect of building design, from conceptual architectural vision to the technical intricacies of civil, electrical, and mechanical engineering. This comprehensive expertise enables us to address every project facet under one roof, ensuring seamless integration across disciplines and eliminating the need for external coordination.

The division employs cutting-edge design software, including AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, and Rhino for architectural design and modeling, and advanced engineering tools like ETABS, SAP2000, and ANSYS for structural analysis. Visualization and rendering are enhanced using state-of-the-art tools such as V-Ray and Lumion, while sustainable design practices are supported by software such as LEED Online, Sefaira, and EnergyPlus.

We combine AAA and EXP's global expertise with our seasoned local personnel who have extensive experience in Nigeria. Our local team will be directly embedded with the client and in close contact with Nigerian authorities, ensuring that every single detail is effectively transmitted and coordinated. This hands-on approach guarantees that the design process is not only successful but also ideally tailored to meet local requirements and client expectations. Through this partnership, we maintain real-time, day-to-day coordination with our international consultants, ensuring that all projects are executed with precision and efficiency. This combination of creative talent, technical prowess, and advanced technology fosters innovative, sustainable, and aesthetically pleasing architectural and engineering solutions, tailored to meet the highest standards of client satisfaction.
